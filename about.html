musicnotebook.app

musicnotebook.app is a free, browser-based music sketchpad that bridges the gap between general note-taking and drawing apps and full notation software.

Start with a blank page of manuscript paper, and write anywhere in the order of musical thought: draw a notehead, add a stem, connect stems with a beam, draw a bar line. Scrub to hear it at your own pace. Scribble markings or make a note. Add a page.

musicnotebook.app is optimized for iPad and also works in desktop browsers. On iPhone, it opens a pocket-sized music notebook with limited features.

Your work is stored in the browser, and you can download, print, or export it as a PDF or PNG. We don't collect or store any data.

Designed for

Composers, songwriters, arrangers, performers, and students.
